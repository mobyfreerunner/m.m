Mubarak Marafa,

A Nigerian Interaction Designer living in Hong Kong. Trained as a New Media Artist specialising in the convergence of Art and Science he is an artist, programmer, and designer who is passionate about building things. Oh, and he has a MDes in Interaction Design. He has spent the last year as a researcher in the PolyU, School of Design.
